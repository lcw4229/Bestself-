# Demand letter template

Customize this template for your firm: replace the bracketed placeholders in
CAPS with your firm's standing details, adjust section order and tone to match
your house style, and add any statutory notice language your jurisdiction
requires. The drafter agent follows this structure and fills in the
matter-specific placeholders from the analyzed documents.

[FIRM NAME]
[FIRM ADDRESS]
[FIRM PHONE] | [FIRM EMAIL]

[DATE]

**VIA [CERTIFIED MAIL / EMAIL]**

[RECIPIENT NAME]
[RECIPIENT TITLE / COMPANY — e.g., claims adjuster and insurer]
[RECIPIENT ADDRESS]

**RE:** Our Client: [CLIENT NAME]
Your Insured: [INSURED NAME]
Claim No.: [CLAIM NUMBER]
Date of Loss: [DATE OF LOSS]

Dear [RECIPIENT NAME]:

## Introduction

This firm represents [CLIENT NAME] in connection with [one-sentence description
of the incident]. This letter constitutes a formal demand for settlement of
[his/her/their] claim.

## Statement of facts

[Chronological narrative of the incident, drawn from the analyzed documents.
Assertive, factual, past tense. Each fact cited to its source document in the
draft.]

## Liability

[Why the recipient's insured/party is responsible: admissions, police findings,
citations issued, witness statements, applicable duty. Facts first; legal
authority only as provided by the attorney.]

## Injuries and treatment

[Injuries sustained, diagnoses, course of treatment, prognosis — from the
medical records. Include treating providers and treatment dates.]

## Damages

| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| [Provider / expense] | $[AMOUNT] |
| [Provider / expense] | $[AMOUNT] |
| Lost wages | $[AMOUNT] |
| **Total special damages** | **$[TOTAL]** |

[Paragraph on general damages: pain and suffering, loss of enjoyment,
ongoing limitations — factual basis from the documents.]

## Demand

Based on the foregoing, we demand payment of **$[DEMAND AMOUNT]** in full
settlement of this claim. This demand remains open until **[DEADLINE DATE —
typically 30 days]**. If we do not receive a satisfactory response by that
date, we are prepared to pursue all available legal remedies without further
notice.

This letter is written in furtherance of settlement negotiations and is
inadmissible for any other purpose.

Sincerely,

[ATTORNEY NAME]
[ATTORNEY TITLE]
[FIRM NAME]

Enclosures: [LIST OF ENCLOSED SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS]
